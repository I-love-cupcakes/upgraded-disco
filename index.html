AI PING PONG









Developed by: Poisha || Game Credit : Ping Pong

Instructions

  1. Keep your laptop screen right
  2. Move yourself approximetly 3 to 4 feet away to the laptop
  3. Move your right wrist in an ascending and descending manner. A red dot should appear on your right wrist.
  4. Now press the play button. Move your wrist to move the pad.
